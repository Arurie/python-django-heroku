Mascot Concept


The main thing I've learned from talking to people about my business idea is that they want a sense of community, ease of use, and a feeling of welcomeness. That's just what this little guy/girl was created for! They will offer users interface help, and can even redirect them to videos if they so prefer.

They will be the size of a small marble in the bottom right corner of the website, and upon clicking, will enlarge to a medium overlay. This is when they will ask the user what they can help them with, or if they just want someone to talk to. This interaction may become like that of an NPC in a game, but for now, these are all just ideas.

They may even be customizable down the line, or have various incarnations due to localization concerns, but for now, they're a lovable circus dog/slug!
